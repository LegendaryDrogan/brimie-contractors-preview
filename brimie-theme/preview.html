Why Brimie?

The name Brimie comes from the brim fish. You can spend all day chasing one big fish, or you can keep catching brim and fill the cooler.

At Brimie Contractors no job is too small. Whether it is a quick inspection repair or a full renovation, every project is approached with the same commitment to doing the work right.

Brimie Contractors is a veteran-owned residential construction company founded by Jason Longmore. Jason served 12 years in the United States Navy, including 8 years as a Naval Parachutist Parachute Rigger and 4 years as a Seabee in Naval Construction.

Inspection resolution estimates and pre-sale renovation assessments

Real estate transactions move quickly and inspection reports often create a short window to resolve repair items before closing. Submit inspection reports and requested resolutions through the form below. Brimie Contractors reviews the information and responds with an estimate within 3–4 business days.

How It Works

1

Submit

Submit the inspection report and requested resolutions through the website form.

2

Review & Estimate

Brimie Contractors reviews the information and provides a repair estimate within 3–4 business days.

3

Complete & Document

If repair work moves forward, Brimie completes the work and provides closing documentation including before-and-after photos and a summary of completed repairs.

Closing Documentation

For inspection repair work that is completed, Brimie Contractors provides a comprehensive completion report that can be shared with buyers and included with closing documentation. The report includes:

  • Summary of completed work
  • Before-and-after photos
  • Clear outline of the repair scope addressed

Renovation Strategy for Investors

Our goal is to help investors focus on improvements that matter most. During project discussions we help identify:

  • Health and safety improvements
  • Functional repairs affecting inspection results
  • Practical upgrades that improve market appeal
  • Cost-effective decisions that protect the overall investment
